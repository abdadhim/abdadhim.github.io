My primary interest is networking / information security. The list below is based around that. I hope this could help you to get off on the right foot. And especially for those who just started learning in this field.

### Online Learning Materials
- [C2C CTF 2021 Learning Materials](https://www.c2c-ctf-2021.org/learningmaterials) - A very good starting point for CTF learners.
- [HACKERSPLOIT](https://hackersploit.org/)
- [HackTheBox Academy](https://academy.hackthebox.com/)
- [Web Security Academy](https://portswigger.net/web-security)
- [Nmap Book Online](https://nmap.org/book/toc.html)
- [Learn to Cloud](https://learntocloud.guide/) - A very good starting point for cloud learners.

### Hands-On Practice
